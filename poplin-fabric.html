Buyer selection guide

Choose A2145, A2134, A9188, A1034 or A9088 by poplin weight, stretch and garment handfeel.

Use A2145 when buyers need 140gsm combed cotton poplin for shirts, dresses, kidswear and uniforms, A1034 for 150gsm light-stretch poplin, A9188 for 125gsm cotton poplin, or A9088 for an 80gsm lightweight poplin route.

Shirt and blouse fabric

A2145 targets 140gsm combed cotton shirt and dress programs; A2134 covers a finer 105gsm 60*60 combed poplin direction; A1034 covers stretch poplin; A9188 covers balanced 125gsm shirt and blouse programs; A9088 is the lighter 80gsm direction.

Dress and apparel programs

Use this product for dresses, lightweight pants, shorts and daily apparel programs that need plain dyed cotton poplin.

FAQ

Common questions before choosing cotton poplin fabric.

Is Poplin Fabric inside Plain Cotton Fabric?

Yes. Poplin Fabric is a child page under Plain Cotton Fabric because A9188 is 100% cotton woven poplin.

What Poplin Fabric products are available now?

A2145 is the 140gsm combed cotton poplin key item, A2134 is the 105gsm 60*60 fine combed poplin option, A9188 is the 125gsm 40s*40s cotton poplin option, A1034 is the 150gsm stretch cotton spandex poplin option, and A9088 is the lighter 80gsm 60*60 cotton poplin option.

What applications should buyers use it for?

Use A1034 for lining, dresses, shirts, blouses and light-stretch apparel; use A2145, A9188 or A9088 when the project does not need spandex stretch.
