Buyer selection guide

Choose tulle by transparency, motif, color effect and final garment use.

For performance and apparel programs, compare handfeel, mesh size, color, width and transparency. For decorative programs, check motif attachment, printed artwork, gradient direction, glitter surface, base tulle color and whether the design suits dresses, skirts, dancewear or garment overlays.

Plain, holiday, glitter or rainbow direction

Plain tulle options such as ZP-7 and YX1046 cover skin tone illusion, wedding dress, bridal and dancewear needs. Holiday tulle options cover seasonal motif briefs, YX2322 covers silver big shiny glitter bridal mesh, SGT001 covers lighter glitter bridal sparkle mesh, YX479 and YX2178 cover rainbow tulle programs, DX129 covers colorful printed star mesh for girls dresses and party apparel, YX2284 covers pearl sequin 3D flower tulle for dresses and skirts, DX54-1 covers lighter sequin 3D flower tulle for fashion dresses and dancewear, and LST009 covers black floral flocked mesh for lingerie, dresses and skirts.

Color and effect planning

For Glitter Tulle Fabric, review glitter density, white or color-card direction, transparency and drape before requesting samples. For Rainbow Tulle Fabric, review color direction, ombre transition, silver dot or glitter surface. For Print Mesh Fabric, confirm artwork scale, foil brightness, print placement and base mesh handfeel. For 3D Tulle Mesh Fabric, confirm embroidery height, pearl and sequin attachment, motif scale and garment placement. For Flocked Mesh Fabric, confirm flocking density, motif scale, adhesion and transparency.
