Buyer selection guide

Choose lace by garment use, material, piece size and edge effect.

For dresses, wedding dresses or special occasion styles, compare white shade, eyelash edge, transparency, handfeel, stretch requirement and whether the piece size fits the planned garment panel. Send the target garment, reference image and quantity for a more useful quotation.

Wedding dress and dress overlay

YX1217-32 is useful when buyers need a white nylon French eyelash lace direction, YX8866 is the stronger glitter lace route for golden sparkle and pearl 3D beads, while YX1174, YX224-1 and ZST001-2 cover heavier or lighter pearl beaded lace options for wedding dresses and bridal gowns.

Fashion dress and dancewear

TB-42, YX1217-32 and YX6042-2 can all be reviewed for decorative dress, dancewear and skirt programs where lace texture and edge effect matter.

FAQ

Common questions before choosing eyelash lace fabric.

What Lace Fabric products are available?

Current lace products include TB-42 French fashion eyelash lace, YX1217-32 white French eyelash lace, YX6042-2 no-stretch Chantilly eyelash lace, YX962 ivory nude skin-color eyelash lace, YX902-1 mocha brown coffee-brown eyelash lace, YX1347-1 multi-color 3D diamond eyelash lace, YX8866 golden glitter lace with embroidery pearl 3D beads, YX1174 white pearl 3D beads sequin lace, YX224-1 3D pearls beads embroidered lace, ZST001-2 pearls 3D tulle mesh lace, YX2439 white eyelet embroidery water soluble lace, YX2429 3D floral cotton voile lace and YX1361 embroidered eyelash lace trim.

What is the MOQ?

MOQ references vary by product: 500 pieces for most eyelash lace and lace trim items, 2 yards for YX8866, 5 yards for YX1174, 10 yards for YX224-1, 10 yards for ZST001-2, and 500 yards for YX2439 and YX2429 water soluble lace products.

What should buyers check before sampling?

Check material, weight, size, white shade or color-card target, glitter brightness, pattern scale, pearl bead density, eyelash edge and intended garment placement.
